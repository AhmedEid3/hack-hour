About Us

Hey there, I'm GPal. I'm the founder of HackHour. Here's the story of how we got started.

I'm a self-taught programmer and know how difficult it's to learn programming. Making consistent progress requires tons of discipline. Often, I was lost trying to figure things alone.

I wished there were short, affordable, live-streamed courses which allowed me to regularly carve out time to learn and build new projects with top-rate instructors and an inspiring community of peers.

That's why I built HackHour. With HackHour, you gain momentum every week and hone your programming. Every week, you are an hour closer to realizing your dreams.

Hope you find HackHour useful as you build new skills and hack projects.If you are interested to partner or have any questions, reach out to me at gpal [AT] hackhour.co . Time's Ticking, Start Hacking! Cya in the next class!
