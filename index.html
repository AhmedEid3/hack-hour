Why HackHour?

Developing new skills is tough. As a team of self-taught programmers, we understand this and designed HackHour to accelerate your path to mastery.

Well-designed beginner friendly pathways to mastery

Get clear explanations and guidance from instructors on how to build new projects so you reach mastery faster.

Clear your doubts instantly during the live class

Have a question? Post it and get answers. Get stuck less and make greater progress.

Join an amazing community which inspires you

Learn with peers who share your interests and inspire you.
